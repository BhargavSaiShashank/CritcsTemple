## ⚖️ 2. Key Philosophical Shifts (Viewer-Critic Optimized)

1.  **The Acting Surge (8%)**: By making Acting a core driver, the system recognizes that performances are the "vessel" of the story. It moves from a side stat to a top-tier influence.
2.  **Emotional Impact as a Pillar (8%)**: This isn't a background stat anymore. A movie that doesn't "move" the audience is now mathematically limited from reaching perfection.
3.  **The Technical Trio (Cinematography 9%, Editing 7%, Sound 6%)**: These are now the heavyweights of the "Making," ensuring the technical craft is respected at the highest level.
4.  **The Writing Split**: Screenplay (9%) slightly edges out Story (8%), emphasizing the **craft of the word** as much as the **power of the idea**.

---

## ⚙️ 3. Implementation Logic (Calculated Distribution)

When applying this to the backend (`utils.py`):
1.  **Weighted Sum**: `Sum(AspectScore * AspectWeight)`.
2.  **Redistribution**: If an aspect is missing, its weight is **redistributed proportionally within its own pillar** to keep the Pillar Totals (28, 26, 22, 12, 12) consistent across every content type.
3.  **Refinements**: Penalties (Foundation/Variance) and Boosts (Synergy) remain active but are now calculated against these high-fidelity weights.
