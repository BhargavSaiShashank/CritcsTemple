## ⚙️ 2. How it Changes the Math

In V7.2, every aspect in **Narrative** is exactly **7.0%** of the score.
In **V8.0**, **Story (15%)** is now **6x more powerful** than the **Opening (2.5%)**.

### Example: "The Masterful Script with a Weak Start"
*   **Story**: 10/10
*   **Opening**: 2/10
*   **V7.2 Logic**: Average of 6.0 (Score drop of **4.0**)
*   **V8.0 Logic**: Weighted score stays high because the 10/10 Story dominates the 2.5% Opening (Score drop of **only ~1.2**).

---

## ⚖️ 3. Handling Missing Aspects (The Redistribution Rule)

When an aspect is not rated:
1.  The system identifies the **Pillar** it belongs to.
2.  The missing weight is redistributed **proportionally** to the other aspects *within the same pillar* only.
3.  This preserves the **Structural DNA** of the review (Narrative stays 35%, even if individual aspects are missing).
