# 📐 V7.2 (Fixed) vs. V8.0 (Dynamic) Refinements

This comparison shows how **Dynamic Refinements** change the score based on the **severity** of the failure or the **depth** of the greatness.

---

## Case 1: The "Soft Failure" (Narrative Score: 6.4)
*The movie is almost good, but has a slight narrative stumble.*

| Logic | Calculation | Deduction | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V7.2 (Fixed)** | Fixed penalty if < 6.5 | **-0.15** | Penalizes the movie heavily for a tiny 0.1 slip-up. |
| **V8.0 (Dynamic)** | `(6.5 - 6.4) * 0.5` | **-0.05** | **Respectful.** A small mistake gets a small penalty. |

---

## Case 2: The "Total Collapse" (Narrative Score: 3.0)
*The story is broken, non-existent, or fundamentally flawed.*

| Logic | Calculation | Deduction | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V7.2 (Fixed)** | Fixed penalty if < 6.5 | **-0.15** | Barely notices the difference between "borderline" and "catastrophe." |
| **V8.0 (Dynamic)** | `(6.5 - 3.0) * 0.5` | **-1.75** | **Smashes.** The score crashes, ensuring the bad film is exposed. |

---

## Case 3: The "Elite Synergy" (All Categories: 9.5+)
*A once-in-a-generation masterpiece where every department is elite.*

| Logic | Calculation | Boost | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V7.2 (Fixed)** | Fixed "Harmony" boost | **+0.07** | A nice tap on the shoulder, but doesn't move the needle much. |
| **V8.0 (Dynamic)** | `(Avg - 8.5) * 0.2` | **+0.20** | **Appreciates.** Pushes the film much closer to the 9.7 ceiling. |

---

### 🗝️ The Philosophy: "The Punishment Matches the Crime"
*   **V7.2** treats every mistake as the same "crime" (-0.15).
*   **V8.0** realizes that a **6.4** is a mistake, but a **3.0** is a disaster. It adjusts the "Hammer" and the "Zenith" to match the actual quality of the film.
