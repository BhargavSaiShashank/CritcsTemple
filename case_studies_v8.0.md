# 📊 Case Studies: Protocol V7.2 vs. V8.0

These simulations demonstrate how the proposed V8.0 changes (Gradient Penalties, Intra-Pillar Weighting, and Gate Removal) would affect different cinematic archetypes.

---

## Example 1: "Neon Void" (The Cold Masterpiece)
**Archetype**: A sci-fi technical marvel with perfect visuals but a "cold" emotional core.

### 📐 19-Aspect Logic DNA
*   **Narrative (35%)**: [Story: 7, Screen: 7, Orig: 8, Open: 8, Climax: 7] → **Avg: 7.4**
*   **Execution (25%)**: [Dir: 9, Acting: 7, Dialog: 7, Depth: 8] → **Avg: 7.75**
*   **Visuals (15%)**: [Cin: 10, Edit: 10, Prod: 10, VFX: 10] → **Avg: 10.0**
*   **Audio (10%)**: [BG: 9, Music: 9, Sound: 9] → **Avg: 9.0**
*   **Soul (15%)**: [Pacing: 6, Emot: 5, Rewatch: 6] → **Avg: 5.67**

### 📊 Verdict Comparison
| System | Score | Archival Signature | Analysis |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V7.2 (Current)** | **7.73** | **GOOD** | Penalized by the -0.05 "Soul Penalty." Capped psychologically by lack of "Heart." |
| **V8.0 (Proposed)** | **7.78** | **ELITE (Architectural)** | Uses Gradient Penalty (calculated as `-0.06' for soul). Category is "Elite (Technical)" instead of a hard gate. |

**V8.0 Advantage**: Recognizes technical perfection (10/10 Visuals) more accurately while still flagging the lack of "Soul" as a structural trait rather than a "failure."

## Example 2: "Deep Roots" (The Rough Diamond)
**Archetype**: An indie drama with a legendary story but weak execution and tiny budget.

### 📐 19-Aspect Logic DNA
*   **Narrative (35%)**: [**Story: 9.5**, **Screen: 9.0**, Orig: 7, Open: 7, Climax: 8] → **Avg: 8.1**
*   **Execution (25%)**: [Dir: 6.0, Acting: 8, Dialog: 8, Depth: 8] → **Avg: 7.5**
*   **Visuals (15%)**: [Cin: 6, Edit: 7, Prod: 5, VFX: 5] → **Avg: 5.75**
*   **Audio (10%)**: [BG: 6, Music: 7, Sound: 6] → **Avg: 6.33**
*   **Soul (15%)**: [Pacing: 7, Emot: 9, Rewatch: 9] → **Avg: 8.33**

### 📊 Verdict Comparison
| System | Score | Archival Signature | Analysis |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **V7.2 (Current)** | **7.41** | **DECENT** | Hits the -0.15 **Foundation Penalty** because Story/Dir avg is borderline or execution is poor. |
| **V8.0 (Proposed)** | **7.89** | **GOOD (Narrative Elite)** | **Intra-Pillar Weighting** prioritizes the 9.5 Story over the 7.0 Opening. Gradient Penalty prevents the "Cliff" drop. |

**V8.0 Advantage**: Prevents a truly great story (9.5) from being buried by minor flaws (5.0 VFX). By weighting **Story** more than **Opening** *within* the Narrative pillar, the movie's true strength is protected.

---

### 🗝️ Key Takeaway
V8.0 stops treating all aspects as equal units. It realizes that **Story** is more important than an **Opening**, and that a **6.4** score should be punished significantly less than a **4.0** score.
